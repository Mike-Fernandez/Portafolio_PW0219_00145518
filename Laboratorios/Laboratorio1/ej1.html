Why inkling can't kill

Whilst many players say that inkilng is one of the best characters in the game it is a probable fact that inkling has trouble to kill. It is true that inkling has one of the best framedatea in the game, meaning that the ending lag on his/her moves is very little, for example if you hit an opponent's shield with your nair you are left with only 2 frames of lag.
